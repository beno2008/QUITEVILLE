About Quiet Ville

Quiet Ville is the fictional cartoon world created by the Saudi content creator Friezy (Feras). It is home to eight main characters whose lives depend on the television inside Friezy's house.

Whenever a new episode begins, the village becomes colorful and full of life. Birdie, Loony and Toony return to their cheerful forms.

When the television is switched off, everything changes. Colors disappear, the world grows silent, and the three characters transform into pale and lifeless versions of themselves, as though the village itself has stopped breathing.
